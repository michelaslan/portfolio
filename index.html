What I've built

Projects

Book Ducks

A full-stack comic book and graphic novel library built with Strapi 5 as the headless CMS and vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks. Users can browse their collection, rate books on a 1–10 scale, manage a personal reading list, and register or log in through Strapi's built-in authentication. Three visual themes (light, dark, and red) are configured from the CMS admin panel and applied dynamically on the frontend — even the duck in the header switches from green to pink for the red theme.
